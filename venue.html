Venue

The 35th IEEE International Symposium on Computer Architecture and High Performance Computing (SBAC-PAD 2023) will be held at the Polytechnic School of the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio Grande do Sul (PUCRS), in Porto Alegre/RS.

The PUCRS campus is located at 6681 Ipiranga Avenue, Partenon, Porto Alegre, RS, Brazil.

For more details about the location within the campus and the access points to PUCRS, please visit the map of the campus.

Accommodation

Due to the limited number of accommodation options around the PUCRS campus, we suggest that the participants of SBAC-PAD 2023 look for hotels in the following neighborhoods: Bom Fim, Centro Histórico, Cidade Baixa, Moinhos de Vento, Petrópolis, Praia de Belas and Santana. These regions are preferred due to the ease of access to the campus, either by public or private transportation, and the higher concentration of hotels and hostels.

Porto Alegre

Porto Alegre is the capital of the state of Rio Grande do Sul. The city is surrounded by the Guaíba Lake on an extension of 70 km of riverfront, in which the city has its most striking postcards. Recently, part of this area has undergone a revitalization process, with the aim of transforming the region and improving the quality of life of the local population. Among the leisure options available along the waterfront are: the "Parque da Orla Moacyr Scliar", the "Parque do Pontal", the "Parque Marinha do Brasil", the "Atracadouro Público da Usina do Gasômetro", the "Cais Mauá", the "Fundação Iberê Camargo", and the nautical clubs.

Besides the waterfront, the city has other leisure and tourist areas and historical attractions, such as the "Mercado Público", the "Casa de Cultura Mário Quintana", and the various parks throughout the city.
